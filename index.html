Ahmedabad University, India

My research lies at the intersection of number theory, quantum computation, and mathematical physics. My work focuses on modular forms, automorphic representations, and the design of scalable quantum algorithms. I am particularly interested in developing mathematically grounded frameworks for quantum computation and exploring their applications in scientific computing, optimization, and signal processing.
